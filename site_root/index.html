I am Andon Tchechmedjiev,
Associate Professor at Institut Mines Telecom in the EuroMov Digital Health in Motion interdisciplinary lab.

My work is at the crossroads of knowledge engineering, machine learning, optimisation and embodied cognitive neuroscience.

Co-PI Semantics and Taxonomy of Human Movement research axis @ EuroMov DHM

Co-animator Data Ecosystem and Governance theme @ IMT Data & AI scientific community

Research

My research interests are in the field of artificial intelligence, human movement science and embodiment as well as medicine. I am particularly interested in the semantics and taxonomy of human movement, the development of knowledge-based systems for human movement analysis, the development of multimodal data fusion methods for human movement analysis, the development of machine learning methods

Past Projects

Designing an automated classification system for intelligent orientation booths that automatically detects the purpose of visit of visitors and steers them appropriately. Project with company ESII.

PractiKPharma was an ANR that studied computer sciences approaches to extract, compare, validate state of the art knowledge in the biomedical domain of Pharmacogneomics (study of how genetics impact drug response phentoypes).
